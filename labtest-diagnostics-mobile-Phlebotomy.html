Mobile Phlebotomy Service
of Labtest Diagnostics

Labtest Diagnostics provides mobile phlebotomy services for patients in the comfort of their own home, we provide blood draw services for group needs, such as

  • Nursing facilities
  • Assisted living facilities
  • Schools for the mentally challenged
  • Company health fairs
  • Company drug screenings
  • Home bound patients

Blood tests are too important to miss or postpone. You can trust and rely on LABTEST DIAGNOSTICS Phlebotomy team for efficient blood draws and prompt scheduling, we are always very caring of all patient needs and treat each patient in a courteous and professional manner.

Hours of operations Technician hours: 5:00AM – 5:00PM Monday through Friday, Special Request for Saturday .
Order for a phlebotomy house call:
If the order is received before 1:00PM during normal business hours, the patient is scheduled for the next business day {if requested}. Any orders received after 1:00PM, will be scheduled within 48 hours.


LABTEST DIAGNOSTICS phlebotomists have many years’ of experience in the mobile phlebotomy field. Because of our expertise and knowledge in phlebotomy, we would like to be given the opportunity to provide mobile phlebotomy service for you.
