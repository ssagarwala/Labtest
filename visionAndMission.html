Vision & Mission
of Labtest Diagnostics

Vision

Labtest Diagnostics would like to become St. Louis Community's undisputed leader in providing excellent, cost efficient laboratory services of the uppermost quality to physicians, assisted living facilities, hospitals, and individuals while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Mission

The mission of Labtest Diagnostics is dedicated to the principles of consistent excellence in esoteric laboratory testing. We are committed to aiding the clinician in early diagnosis and continued disease management through the design of laboratory profiles and panels that provide the ordering physician an array of preselected tests. These tests assist to resolve the various clinical problems faced in practice at the most cost-effective manner possible.


Labtest Diagnostics uses state of the art technology performed solely by an accredited laboratory and experienced professionals. Labtest Diagnostic’s staff treats our clients, their staff and patients with the respect they deserve. Client Services Representatives at Labtest Diagnostics are ready to do “whatever it takes” to satisfy our clients.


We shall achieve excellence in diagnostic testing, information and services to become the area's most valued company to patients, customers, colleagues, investors, business partners, and the communities where we work and live.
